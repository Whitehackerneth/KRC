About Us

Welcome to the Kannangara College Mathugama Rubik's Club (KRC)! We are a passionate community of Rubik's Cube enthusiasts dedicated to promoting the art of solving twisty puzzles. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced cuber, we provide a welcoming space for you to learn, teach, and improve your solving skills. Join us for regular meetups, workshops, and competitions to connect with like-minded individuals and challenge yourself to solve puzzles faster. If you have any questions or want to get involved, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. We're thrilled to have you as part of our Rubik's Cube community at Kannangara College Mathugama!

FAQ

The Rubik's Cube club is a gathering of enthusiasts who share a common interest in solving Rubik's Cubes and other twisty puzzles. We come together to learn, practice, and enjoy the challenges of speedcubing.

No prior knowledge is required. The club is open to everyone, from beginners to advanced cubers. We provide resources and support for those who are just starting out.

Club meetings include a variety of activities such as solving sessions, tutorials, and friendly competitions. We also discuss new solving techniques, algorithms, and puzzles.
